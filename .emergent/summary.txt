**original_problem_statement:**
The user wants to build a mobile application for their construction company, which will function as a digital timesheet (Werkbon).

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
1.  **User Authentication:**
    *   Employees will log in using their company email addresses.
    *   There must be a distinction between 'Admin' and 'Worker' roles.
    *   Admins should have access to a settings/management panel ('Beheer').
    *   Workers should only be able to create and manage their timesheets.

2.  **Timesheet (Werkbon) Creation:**
    *   Select a client and a worksite (werf). The list of worksites should be filtered based on the selected client.
    *   The form should be based on a weekly view, automatically populating dates when a week number is entered.
    *   Log effectief gewerkte uren (effective hours worked) for each team member per day.
    *   Log KM afstand (kilometer distance) per day.
    *   Include a text area for uitgevoerde werken (work performed).
    *   Include a text area for gebruikte extra materialen (extra materials used).
    *   Ability to mark days with abbreviations for non-working reasons: Z (ziek/sick), V (verlof/leave), BV (betaald verlof/paid leave), BF (betaald feestdag/paid holiday).
    *   Calculate and display total hours per employee for the week.
    *   Calculate and display a grand total of all hours for the team for the week.

3.  **Admin Management ('Beheer') Panel:**
    *   Manage Teams: Create/edit teams and assign employees to them.
    *   Manage Clients: Add/edit client information, including a specific hourly rate ().
    *   Manage Worksites (Werven).
    *   Manage Company Settings: A place to input company details like address, which will be used in the PDF.

4.  **Signature & PDF Generation:**
    *   A feature for the client's representative to sign the timesheet directly on the mobile device.
    *   Generate a professional-looking PDF of the completed and signed timesheet.
    *   The PDF must include the Smart Tech logo, all the timesheet details, the signature, and company information.

5.  **Email Functionality:**
    *   After signing, the PDF should be automatically emailed to both the company (a predefined address) and the client.
    *   The email sent to the client should include the total cost (total hours * client's hourly rate) and a legal disclaimer stating that the invoice is considered approved if no objections are raised within a week.
